About UTD Link

UTD Link is a platform designed to bring all academic, professional, and campus opportunities into one easy-to-access place. Students often miss out on events, internships, research openings, and volunteer programs simply because information is scattered across emails, flyers, and department pages. Our goal is to fix that by creating a single hub that connects students, organizations, and faculty.

Our Mission

We want to improve student engagement at UTD by making opportunities easy to find and easy to participate in. Whether you're looking for an internship, a research project, a student organization event, or an on-campus job, UTD Link gives you the tools to explore, filter, and register effortlessly.

Why UTD Link?

  • Opportunities are currently scattered across different platforms.
  • Students often miss deadlines or events due to poor visibility.
  • Excel sheets and emails cannot handle complex relationships or large datasets.
  • Faculty and organizations need a simple way to manage postings and track participation.

A centralized relational database makes it easier to keep information organized, accurate, and scalable as more users and opportunities join the system.

Who Is UTD Link For?

UTD Link is built for everyone in the campus community:

Students

Browse, filter, and sign up for internships, research, events, and more.

Faculty & Organizers

Post opportunities, track student participation, and manage event details.
